I’m a Game Designer with 4 years of experience working in a game studio as well as personal projects, focusing on Gameplay design while expanding into Level and Progression design. I enjoy solving challenges and I’m always looking to grow, both as a game designer and as a person.

Citizen Conflict

In Citizen Confict (a third-person hero shooter), I was responsible for overseeing weapon behavior and balance, designing gameplay mechanics related to movement, combat and progression systems. I also contributed to level design, handling blockouts, playtesting and iterating for two maps that still remain in the active map pool. In earlier stages of development I contributed to general game design and helping shape vision for Citizen Confict.

Aneemate
